Computer Science (100 marks)

📋 Paper Scheme

MCQs: 20 marks (20 questions, 1 mark each)

Short Questions: 40 marks (8 out of 10, 5 marks each)

Long Questions: 40 marks (2 out of 3, 20 marks each)

Total Theory = 100 | Practical = 100 (included separately)

🔗 Common Pairing (Long Qs)

  • Pair 1: Data Basics + DBMS (Ch 1 & 2)
  • Pair 2: Basic Concepts of OOP + Classes & Objects (Ch 3 & 4)
  • Pair 3: File Handling + Functions (Ch 5 & 6)

⭐ Topic Weightage (High to Low)

HIGH (70% marks)

Chapter 1: Data Basics | Chapter 2: DBMS | Chapter 3: OOP | Chapter 4: Classes & Objects | Chapter 5: File Handling | Chapter 6: Functions & Pointers

MEDIUM (20% marks)

Chapter 7: Arrays | Chapter 8: Graphics & Animation

LOW (10% marks)

Chapter 9: Internet & WWW (mostly MCQs)

💡 Strategy: Memorize definitions, diagrams, and short Qs. Long Qs come from Ch 1‑6. Past papers 80% repetition.

Mathematics (100 marks)

📝 Paper Scheme & Pairing

MCQs: 20 marks (20 MCQs from all chapters)

Short Questions: 40 marks (8 out of 10, each 5 marks)

Long Questions: 40 marks (2 out of 3 pairs, 20 marks each pair)

🔁 Long Question Pairings (Typical Multan Board)

  • Pair I: Functions & Limits (Ch 1) + Integration (Ch 3)
  • Pair II: Differentiation (Ch 2) + Differential Equations (Ch 4)
  • Pair III: Vectors (Ch 5) + Analytic Geometry (Ch 6)

⚠️ Zero‑level student advice:

Focus only on Integration, Differential Equations, Vectors, Analytic Geometry, Derivatives basics. Skip complex proofs & rare exercises. Solve past 10 years questions directly.

🎯 Chapter Weightage (Marks distribution)

HIGH (60‑70 marks)

Ch 1: Functions & Limits | Ch 2: Differentiation | Ch 3: Integration | Ch 4: Differential Equations | Ch 5: Vectors | Ch 6: Analytic Geometry

MEDIUM (20‑25 marks)

Ch 7: Linear Inequalities | Ch 8: Conics (partial)

LOW (5‑10 marks)

Ch 9: Probability | Ch 10: Regression (mostly short Qs or MCQs)

💡 Goal: 60‑70 marks → solve at least 4 long questions pairs (2 from high weightage) and 15‑18 MCQs.

Physics (100 marks)

📋 Paper Structure

MCQs: 20 marks (20 questions)

Short Questions: 30 marks (6 out of 8, 5 marks each)

Long Questions: 50 marks (2 out of 3, 25 marks each: part a: numerical, part b: derivation)

🔁 Typical Pairing (Long Qs)

  • Pair 1: Electrostatics (Ch 11) + Current Electricity (Ch 12)
  • Pair 2: Electromagnetism (Ch 13) + Electromagnetic Induction (Ch 14)
  • Pair 3: Alternating Current (Ch 15) + Physics of Solids (Ch 16)

⭐ Important Topics & Weightage

HIGH (70 marks)

Ch 11: Electrostatics (Gauss law, capacitors)
Ch 12: Current Electricity (Kirchhoff’s laws, Wheatstone)
Ch 13: Electromagnetism (force on moving charge, galvanometer)
Ch 14: Electromagnetic Induction (Faraday’s law, transformer)

MEDIUM (20 marks)

Ch 15: A.C Circuits (LCR, resonance) | Ch 16: Solids (energy bands, semiconductor)

LOW (10 marks)

Ch 17: Electronics (mostly MCQs/short)

💡 Numerical survival: practice 10 top numericals from past papers (Electrostatics, current electricity, electromagnetism).

English (100 marks)

Paper Scheme (Multan Board pattern)

  • Essay / Paragraph (15 marks)
  • Application / Letter (10 marks)
  • Translation (Urdu → English) (15 marks)
  • Summary of poem / Story (10 marks)
  • Grammar & Syntax (20 marks)
  • Comprehension (15 marks)
  • Dialogue / Idioms (15 marks)

⚡ High‑Yield Topics

✍️ Essays: My aim in life, A visit to historical place, Importance of education, Global warming

📬 Letters: Application to principal, letter to friend about studies

📖 Stories: A friend in need, Honesty is best policy, Union is strength

🌐 Translation: practice 5 passages from past papers

💡 Target 65‑70 marks: memorize 5 essays, 3 letters, 3 stories, and practice translation daily.

Pakistan Studies (50 marks)

📌 Paper Pattern

  • MCQs: 10 marks
  • Short Questions: 20 marks (4 out of 6)
  • Long Question: 20 marks (1 out of 2)

📚 High‑weightage Chapters

Chapter 1: Ideology of Pakistan (short Qs)

Chapter 2: Pakistan Movement (long Qs often from 1940‑1947)

Chapter 3: Land & Climate (MCQs)

Chapter 4: Economy & Resources (short Qs)

💡 Target 40+ marks → memorize 15 short Qs and 2 long Qs (Lahore Resolution, Two‑Nation Theory).

⚡ 45‑Day Crash Plan (Zero to 500+)

Daily 10‑hour schedule + past paper focus

📆 Phase 1: Days 1‑15

Math: Derivatives, Integration basics
Physics: Electrostatics, Current Electricity (numericals + theory)
Computer: Ch 1‑4 definitions & short Qs
English/Urdu: Essay/letter formats, ghazal summaries

📚 Phase 2: Days 16‑30

Math: Differential Equations, Vectors, solve 5 past papers
Physics: Electromagnetism, Induction + derivations
Computer: Ch 5‑6 long Qs + diagrams
Past papers: start solving full subject papers

🎯 Phase 3: Days 31‑45

Revision mode: one full past paper daily (all subjects)
Math/Physics: formula sheets, repeated numericals
Computer/Eng/Urdu/Pak Studies: memorize key content
Last 5 days: only revise notes, formulas, essays

🚨 Crucial: Solve last 10 years’ past papers for Math & Physics — 70% questions repeat. Do not touch new topics after Day 35.
